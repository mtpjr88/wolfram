Wolfram Tru-Arc single-ended lamps are engineered to be structurally superior and more durable than any other single-ended lamps on the market. Wolfram's single-ended lamps use tungsten dowel-rods sealed into a ceramic base, which provides unparalleled support when the lamp is installed into the fixture, thus creating a stronger seal than the commonly used ribbon technique.

Double Ended Lamps

For use with fresnel style fixtures. Wolfram Tru-Arc double-ended lamps are specially processed during construction to extend lamp life and ensure arc stability. Each individual lamp is also computer-analyzed for the proper chemical fill, guaranteeing the color temperature and operating characteristics to be the most consistent in the industry.
